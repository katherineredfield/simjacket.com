The Issue

Practice on peers is often too easy to palpate and scan. Early exposure can be inconsistent, and difficult placements may cluster with a few learners rather than being standardized.

The Innovation

The simulation jacket recreates a more challenging exam on real, live anatomy while remaining simple to build, low-cost, scalable, and easy to insert into a rapid-cycle teaching session.
